# Engaging Rationale Design - Clear, Lively, Actionable

**Goal:** Replace robotic, generic outputs with **vibrant, clear storytelling** that motivates students while being rigorously honest.

---

## ‚ùå What to Avoid (OLD STYLE - Robotic & Generic)

```
Score: 74/110

**Placement Potential**: UC Davis, UC Irvine, Boston University, Wisconsin

Competitive for many UCs. To strengthen for UCSD/Michigan level (80+), aim for
higher academic rigor.

**To Reach HYPSM Level (100+)**: 10+ APs mostly 5s + 3.9+ GPA
```

**Problems:**
- Dry and corporate ("Placement Potential", "Competitive for")
- Uses unclear acronyms (HYPSM - most students won't know this!)
- Feels like a form letter
- No personality or encouragement
- Just lists facts without context
