**What It Detects**:
- Barriers disclosed (educational, financial, personal)
- Constraints explained (time, resources, access)

- First-generation/underrepresented status (when relevant)

**Current Issue**:
- Our rubric assumes context is disclosed elsewhere in application
- But PIQs are often where students **explain circumstances**
- Not detecting this = missing major UC evaluation criteria
